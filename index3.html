Our Story

Connecting rural traditions with urban lives

How It All Began

Gaav Se was born from a simple realization during a visit to a remote village in Rajasthan. We discovered families preserving century-old recipes and techniques, creating products of unparalleled purity and quality, yet struggling to find markets beyond their immediate surroundings.

Meanwhile, urban consumers were yearning for authentic, chemical-free products but had no access to these rural treasures. Gaav Se became the bridge between these two worlds.

Today, we work with over 200 artisan families across 15 states, bringing their craftsmanship to your doorstep while ensuring they receive fair compensation and recognition for their skills.

Empowering Rural India

At Gaav Se, we measure our success not just by products sold, but by lives transformed. Our direct-from-artisan model ensures that 85% of what you pay goes directly to the makers, compared to just 15-20% in traditional retail models.

We've helped establish women's self-help groups, provided training in quality standards, and created sustainable livelihoods that allow artisans to continue living in their villages while earning fair wages.

Your support helps preserve India's rich culinary and wellness traditions while creating economic opportunities in rural areas.
